## Summary
The **Doji family** were the descendants of the children of [Doji](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Doji "Doji"and [Kakita](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Kakita "Kakita") who took their mother's name. They were the Champions of the Crane, and are frequently, though not exclusively, the [Emerald Champions](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Emerald_Champion "Emerald Champion") as well. The Doji hosted the Crane's [courtier](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Courtier "Courtier") and [magistrate](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Magistrate "Magistrate") schools, although many of their number trained in the [Kakita](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Kakita_family "Kakita family") schools also.
