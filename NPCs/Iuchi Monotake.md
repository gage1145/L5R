## Overview 
A battalion commander at the gates between Scorpion and Unicorn lands. Signed travel papers for the party before they entered the Unicorn plains. Hates [[Seppun Haruhisa]]. Recommended the party meet [[Matsu Koshe]] in [[The City of the Rich Frog]]. Was attending his first-ever Winter Court at the time of Session 27; [[Doji Gaoxing]] got the impression he enjoys the politics but finds the repetition tiring.
