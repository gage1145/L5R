## Overview 
Right hand man and chief advisor to [[Seppun Haruhisa]]. Served as an imperial envoy during the Badger lands crisis. Was meeting with [[Ichiro Toshekatsu]] at [[Shiro Ichiro]] when the party arrived — berated him loudly about pride, treason, and chaos, demanding accountability for the disorder in Badger lands. It was Kuranosuke and [[Ichiro Masakatsu]] together who ordered Toshekatsu to perform seppuku. His presence in Badger lands complicated the [[Kyōtsū no michi]]'s ability to provide support to the party.
