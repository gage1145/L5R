Daimyo of Sabishii Province and the apparent founder and organizer of the [[Kyōtsū no michi]]. A calculating Crane politician. Hosted the party at Ranshudin Castle at the outset of the campaign, where he revealed the conspiracy and assigned the group its first directive: hunt down the four members of the [[Gokenishi]]. He is Daidoji Haruki's cousin.

Communicates through letters and intermediaries as the campaign progresses. After the party's success in Badger lands, he sent a letter through [[Ichiro Jinsuke]] arranging for the group's attendance at a tournament in Phoenix lands hosted by [[Asaku Shizo]] at [[Shiro Aokiro Oku]]. Primary point of contact with the conspiracy for [[Doji Gaoxing]] and [[Doji Junji]].
