## Overview 
The lord of [[Wachimasu]] and long-standing rival of [[Daidoji Haruki]] over which of them runs the better border town. A large, volatile man who launched into lengthy tirades against the Akodo family and the Crab clan when even mildly accused during the party's investigation into the stolen swords of [[Seppun Daiori]]. Vehemently denied any involvement, and no evidence tied him to the theft.
