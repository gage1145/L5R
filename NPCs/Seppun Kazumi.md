## Overview 
![](https://upload.wikimedia.org/wikipedia/commons/thumb/5/5c/Saigo-no-Tsubone2.JPG/1200px-Saigo-no-Tsubone2.JPG)

A woman of the imperial Seppun family with long dark hair. Stole the ceremonial swords of [[Seppun Daiori]] from [[Daidoji Haruki]]'s estate — not out of malice, but to prevent their use. She knew the swords were haunted by Daiori's angry spirit and intended to bury them. Was tracked by the party to a hill north of [[Wachimasu]], where she shattered the swords with an axe before they could be recovered. Had a bandage on her left hand at the time of the encounter. Submitted herself peacefully after explaining her reasoning; the party accepted her explanation and let her go.
