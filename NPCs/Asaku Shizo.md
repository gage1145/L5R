## Overview 
A minor Phoenix aristocrat and lord of [[Shiro Aokiro Oku]] in Phoenix lands. Is hosting a tournament to which the party was summoned by [[Daidoji Kentarō]] via a letter delivered through [[Ichiro Jinsuke]]. The tournament is approximately seven weeks from the party's departure from Badger lands. [[Koganshi Onotatsu]] was visibly hesitant about returning to Phoenix lands in connection with this invitation.
