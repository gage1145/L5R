## Overview 
Cousin of [[Daidoji Kentarō]] and governor of [[Tochigi]]. Provided the party with papers to travel through Scorpion lands. Was hosting a tournament arranged by [[Seppun Ayumu]] when the ceremonial swords of [[Seppun Daiori]] — gifted to him by Ayumu — were stolen from his estate in the night. The theft was ultimately the work of [[Seppun Kazumi]], who destroyed the swords to prevent their cursed spirit from being wielded.

Has an ongoing rivalry with [[Matsu Samano]], lord of neighboring [[Wachimasu]], over which of them runs the better border town. Exiled [[Akodo Yakisoba]] from his lands as punishment for stealing a horse during the investigation into the stolen swords.
