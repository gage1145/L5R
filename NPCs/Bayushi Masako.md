## Overview 
The Scorpion clan champion and head of the Scorpion clan. Despite the party killing one of his samurai (the ronin [[Kuma Mitomoro]]) on the road through Scorpion lands, he chose to handle it pragmatically rather than with force. When [[Togashi Hanzo]] navigated the labyrinth at [[Kyuden Bayushi]] and apologized in person, Masako accepted and granted papers through Unicorn and Dragon lands. He then used [[Bayushi Ogoro]] to arrange the party's "official death" — staging a false murder scene to let them slip out of Scorpion territory with clean records.

Invited Hanzo to the Bayushi Festival the following year. Has a strained relationship with [[Ide Chokekami]].
