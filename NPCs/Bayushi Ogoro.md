## Overview 
A Scorpion bushi in [[Kyuden Bayushi]]. Arrested the party on arrival and led them not toward the castle but out to a rural area, where he staged an elaborate false murder scene — beheading a pig farmer and having two of his own guards cut each other down — to create the impression that the party had been executed. He then told the group to run, effectively granting them "official" dead status while letting them escape. It became clear this was sanctioned by [[Bayushi Masako]] as a way to cleanly resolve the situation.
