## Overview 
A retainer for [[Ichiro Masakatsu]] who defended him upon his death. She was trained in the Hare ways, and [[Usagi Haku]] recognized her because of that. She killed herself.

Yōjimbō to [[Ichiro Masakatsu]] who fucked up [[Akodo Yakisoba]] and [[Kuni Yoshiyuki]] and committed seppuku after being poisoned by Yoshiuki. Trained at the [[Usagi Dojo]].
