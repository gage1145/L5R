## Overview 
A lord in [[The City of the Rich Frog]] whom [[Iuchi Monotake]] recommended the party meet. Built like a bushi but carries himself and dresses as a courtier. Hosted the party at his mansion in the city. Gossips lightly about Winter Court. Has enough political standing to know what's happening across the region.

Tasked the party with investigating a brief distress missive — *"Need help. Send it quickly."* — received from [[Akoma Takashi]] in the village of [[Mushi Mura]], which holds a yearly festival during the first week of Togashi.
