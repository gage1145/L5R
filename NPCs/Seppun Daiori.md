## Overview 
A historical Seppun hero who led the Seppun forces against the Scorpion a century ago. His ceremonial swords — noted by [[Togashi Hanzo]] to be Scorpion relics from that conflict — were gifted to [[Daidoji Haruki]] by [[Seppun Ayumu]]. Daiori's angry spirit was said to inhabit the blades, which made them dangerous. The swords were stolen from Haruki's estate by [[Seppun Kazumi]] and destroyed to put his spirit to rest.
