## Overview
A Unicorn liaison with close ties to Unicorn champion [[Shinjo Tamogen]], and a covert member of the [[Kyōtsū no michi]] alongside her lord [[Ide Tadamoto]]. Met the party at a shrine in [[Iuchi Gozaiman]]'s village before they entered Badger lands. Provided key intel: Badger lands are on the verge of civil war, the [[Path of Woe]] is the only entry or exit point, and Badger champion [[Ichiro Toshekatsu]] would not be heartbroken if [[Ichiro Masakatsu]] died. Waited at the Path of Woe for up to two months for the party's return.

Has a strained relationship with [[Bayushi Masako]]. Her lord was initially unaware of her Kyōtsū no michi involvement. After the party's return through Unicorn lands, she revealed both her and Tadamoto's membership in the conspiracy, noting that [[Moto Kuranosuke]]'s presence in Badger lands had complicated their plans to provide more direct support.
