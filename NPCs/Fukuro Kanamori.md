## Overview 
A sifu running a dojo in [[The City of the Rich Frog]]. [[Usagi Haku]] was drawn to it because the exterior resembled a Usagi dojo, but did not recognize the techniques being practiced inside. When Haku approached him, Kanamori noted that it was unusual to see a Hare so far from home and asked *"What dangers bring you here, Hare?"* He is a member of the [[Owl Clan]] — a clan Haku had never heard of. His dojo has no connection to the Usagi school.
