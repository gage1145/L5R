The half-brother of [[Emperor Hantei XXXVI]]. His complete disregard for customs or decency threaten to bring shame to the Chrysanthemum Throne and potentially destabilize the entire Emerald Empire. Member of the [[Gokenishi]] and the ultimate target of the [[Kyōtsū no michi]] conspiracy. His crimes against the Hare Clan were the catalyst for the conspiracy's formation. [[Moto Kuranosuke]] serves as his right hand man and imperial envoy. The party has not yet moved directly against him.
