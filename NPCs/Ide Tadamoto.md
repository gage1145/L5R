## Overview 
Daimyo of the Ide family and the Unicorn Clan. Met with the party privately in Shiroi Kishi Mura, asking them about their time in Badger lands before the group revealed the full scope of the [[Kyōtsū no michi]] conspiracy to him. He dismissed them after hearing it. His retainer [[Ide Chokekami]] later confirmed that he is himself a member of the conspiracy — his apparent surprise was likely a calculated response. His presence and [[Moto Kuranosuke]]'s simultaneously in Badger lands had complicated the Kyōtsū no michi's ability to provide direct aid to the party.
