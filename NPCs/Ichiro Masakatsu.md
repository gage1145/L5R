A member of the [[Gokenishi]] and lord in Badger lands. A brutal man who extorted villages with unreasonable taxes and threatened to raze those who refused — including [[Ogana Owari]]. Based out of [[Shiro Bagato]] in the mountains.

Used his personal retainer [[Ichiro Shingen]] as a body double. The party initially killed Shingen during a mission in Badger lands, believing him to be Masakatsu — a mistake that led directly to the forced seppuku of Badger daimyo [[Ichiro Toshekatsu]]. The real Masakatsu fled early during the [[Battle of The Claws of the Badger]] and was eventually run down and killed by [[Togashi Hanzo]] with fire breath. Owned a high-quality sword forged by [[Tsi Konochiro]], a prominent swordsmith in [[Otosan Uchi]], likely gifted to him. Had close ties to the imperial envoy [[Moto Kuranosuke]].
