## Overview 
Presented as a Crane samurai stranded in Scorpion lands — in reality a Scorpion agent in disguise. She was part of a group (alongside two Lions, a Crab, and a dead Fox) that claimed to be hunting [[Lady Night]] for [[Daidoji Kentarō]] and had lost the trail. They shared intel about Lady Night before revealing they had tricked the party and launched an ambush. The group defeated them. Her last words before [[Togashi Hanzo]] incinerated her: *"If I were you, I would beg forgiveness from [[Bayushi Masako]]."*
