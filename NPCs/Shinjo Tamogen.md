## Overview 
The Unicorn Clan champion and daimyo. [[Ide Chokekami]] has close associations with him and is based near his court, though he may not have initially been aware of her involvement in the [[Kyōtsū no michi]]. [[Ide Chokekami]] later confirmed that he is, in fact, a member of the conspiracy alongside her.
