Member of the [[Gokenishi]] and the third primary target of the [[Kyōtsū no michi]]. Associated with blood magic and believed to be a member or retainer of a Phoenix vassal clan. Has been in the Shadowlands multiple times. The group sent by [[Daidoji Kentarō]] to track her (including [[Daidoji Atabashi]]'s party) lost her trail before turning on the party in Scorpion lands. Last rumored to have been in [[Ishigaki Province]] in Crab territory.
