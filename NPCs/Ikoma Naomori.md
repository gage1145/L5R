## Overview 
A wounded Lion bushi whose spirit the party encountered in the mountains of Badger lands — [[Akodo Yakisoba]] recognized his name. He fell in love with a geisha named Gai while still engaged to the sister of Phoenix shugenja [[Isawa Tenzin]]; Tenzin killed Gai, and Naomori could not best him in the duel that followed. His spirit sought revenge and appeared to the party still wounded. When Yakisoba offered to help him, Naomori's true nature as a spirit was revealed and he spirited Yakisoba away.
