## Overview 
Commander of a Seppun rifleman battalion marching to [[The Claws of the Badger]], ostensibly to defend [[Ichiro Masakatsu]]'s forces. [[Doji Gaoxing]] intercepted and stalled his unit, informing him that a battle was already underway and that "the daimyo is correcting a situation." Gaoxing successfully delayed his battalion long enough that the outcome of the battle was decided before Surumi's troops could intervene.
