## Overview 
A daitengu who dwells at a shrine in the caldera of the mountains in Badger lands. Had prior dealings with [[Ichiro Masakatsu]] before the party arrived. Both [[Akodo Yakisoba]] and [[Doji Junji]] challenged him to duels seeking to become his students — he defeated both of them decisively.

Later appeared unbidden on the road to [[Shiro Ichiro]] and shared a koan with the party: a story about a young retainer on his way to enlightenment who was provoked and struck by a monk repeatedly until he finally killed him. At his funeral, the abbot asked "why?" — the retainer answered that he "had not yet begun to walk the sacred road."
