City: "[[Ranshudin]]"

Daimyo of Sabishii Province.  A member of the [[Kyōtsū no michi]] and host to the party at his castle of Ranshudin Castle during the first scenes of the campaign.  A Crane Clan politician.  Primary point of contact with the conspiracy for Malachi and Joey's characters.
