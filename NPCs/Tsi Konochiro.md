## Overview 
A high-end swordsmith based in [[Otosan Uchi]]. [[Doji Gaoxing]] recognized his maker's mark on [[Ichiro Masakatsu]]'s personal sword after the Battle of the Claws of the Badger — the sword was heavier than expected and was likely gifted to Masakatsu by a patron.
