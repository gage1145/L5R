## Overview 
Nephew and heir to [[Ichiro Toshekatsu]]. Led the Badger forces in the siege of [[Shiro Bagato]] against [[Ichiro Masakatsu]]'s army, and commanded the Badger forces at the [[Battle of The Claws of the Badger]]. Following Toshekatsu's seppuku, became the de facto leader of the Badger clan.

After Masakatsu's death, considered performing seppuku himself out of shame for allowing the clan to fall under Masakatsu's influence. Was talked out of it by [[Doji Gaoxing]], who argued that his people needed him as a symbol of hope, not another death. Handed over a letter from [[Daidoji Kentarō]] that had been written weeks in advance, commending the party and arranging their attendance at a tournament in Phoenix lands hosted by [[Asaku Shizo]].
