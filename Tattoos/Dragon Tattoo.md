#### Summary
The dragon is a fearsome tattoo, and contains frightening power waiting to be unleashed.

#### Mechanics
- As a Complex Action, you may breathe fire in a cone, equal to ten feet plus your School Rank in length, and five feet wide at the end.
- This fire deals damage to all in its area of effect, with a DR equal to your Fire ring keep Fire. You take damage equal to your Fire ring upon each use.
