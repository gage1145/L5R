### Returning the head
We pass a camp stealthily, and we glance back, and the camp is being massacred by a looming figure. We continue to [[Shiro Ichiro]]. Ahead of us, Omoshi is standing in the road. He looks so fucking cool. He asks to tell us a story.

He tells a story about a young retainer on his way to enlightenment. A young monk knew this and met him on the road and hit him. The retainer refused to draw the weapon, and the monk kicked him. He demanded to know why he was attacking. The monk hit him on the head, and he killed the monk. The abbot at the funeral asked, "why?". The retainer said he "had not yet begun to walk the sacred road."
