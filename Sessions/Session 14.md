## Overview
### The Savannah Battle
We picked up in the midst of battle with the monks. Two are already dead, another is fleeing, and the last is deep in combat with Haku. We burned the corpses and took the living monk back to the castle. We killed that guy anyway before getting any information.

### Back at the Village
[[Iuchi Gozaiman]] thanks us for fucking up those monks and provides us some clothes for passing into Badger lands. 

### The Road to Badger Lands
We eventually come across a monk named [[Yogo]], He is on a personal journey through Unicorn lands, seeking things that he should learn. I miss him already.

We continue closer to the [[Path of Woe]]. It starts getting pretty cold, and the whole group took damage. Eventually we start seeing snow. It's cold af.

One night while we are warming ourselves by the campfire, a couple figures approach. One is clearly a bushi. Her armor is a light green, and Haku recognizes her as [[Mantis Clan]]. She has been serving as a bodyguard for the merchant she's traveling with. His name is [[Hikaru]]. Her name is [[Yuritomo Kyoko]]. We welcome them and Hikaru shares some of his tea. 

They came from the village [[Ogana Owari]]. [[Ichiro Masakatsu]] has been threatening unreasonable taxes, and to raze the village. Ichiro lives in a castle further in the mountains in [[Shiro Bagato]].

She tells us that the badgers love heavy weapons.
