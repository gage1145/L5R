## Overview

### The Path to [[The City of the Rich Frog]]
Half the party (Hanzo, Junji, Gaoxing, and Haku) heads to [[Shiro Shinjo]] to find a boat into Phoenix lands. We find a captain that is willing to take us for 3 bu.

Once we get to the city, we start looking for our respective schools. Gaoxing wants to know about the illegal gun trade being smuggled through the city. He writes a letter to [[Daidoji Kentarō]] expressing his concern about the black market and his suspicion of an approaching uprising.

After writing that, Gaoxing finds a bureaucrat and asks about [[Matsu Koshe]], the man that [[Iuchi Monotake]] told us to meet when we arrived here. The bureaucrat also mentioned that we could do a favor for [[Hoshe Mitsu]], the magistrate of the city.

Hanzo finds a house that he knows that Togashi monks occasionally stay in.

Haku finds an Usagi dojo, but he doesn't really recognize the techniques they're practicing. He approaches a man who he thinks is the sifu of this dojo. The man introduces of himself as [[Fukuro Kanamori]]. He scans Usagi and says that it's unusual to see an Usagi so far from home.

**"What dangers bring you here, Hare?"**

Haku says that his family sent him here to experience the world.

It turns out that he's a member of the [[Owl Clan]], which Haku had never heard of. Turns out this dojo is not in fact an Usagi dojo.

### The Lion, the Crab, and the Phoenix
Yakisoba, Yoshiyuki, and Onotatsu, while travelling to Shiro Shinjo, are ambushed. They both heard a gunshot; the bullet narrowly missing Yoshiyuki.

Yoshiyuki and Yakisoba manages to vivisect the female attacker and consider taking her gun. Unfortunately, this gun is very recognizably an Imperial weapon. Of course, they do test out the weapon! It's fucking sick. 

**It appears the Imperials are now hunting us...**

Under the woman's armor, she is wearing peasant clothing...

**Is this really an Imperial assassin?*

They eventually arrive in Shiro Shinjo and buy passage to the frog town.

### Meeting [[Matsu Koshe]]
The four in the city finally meet Matsu Koshe in his mansion. He's built like a bushi, but carries himself and dresses like a courtier. He offers us a place to stay.

Gaoxing inquires how [[Iuchi Monotake]] is doing in his first ever [[Winter Court]]. He gossips a bit about the Winter Court. We get the sense that he likes the games, but he's kind of tired of it. He's been to it enough times.

### Let's Go Gambling!
While everyone else visits the magistrate, Yoshiyuki goes gambling! He meets a woman with her incisors filed into points, and gold teeth scattered throughout her mouth. He recognizes her as [[Shark Clan]]. She invites him over, and grossly disrespected Yoshiyuki by indirectly insinuating he is of a lower rank. Damn these guys are fucking rowdy! Yoshiyuki says he'll play if there's an open seat.

**There's always an open seat, but we're playing with a handicap. So, you gotta catch up!**

Yoshiyuki tries to catch up by chugging shochu. He wins a round, and the woman says that he has to now buy the next round! Yoshiyuki orders a round of fight milk (araka) lol. One guy vomits this disgusting excuse for a libation.

Since he won again, he had to buy the next round...again. He ordered another less (but still) disgusting drink. He's holding his liquor while the others are lagging behind!

He decides it's time to go, and they get upset and accuse him of cheating. They tell him that he owes them a new game, and tell him to visit them in [[Yage no-Kagamora]].

### The Magistrate's Court
Junji meets the commander of the border, [[Akodo Kinsume]]. 

Hanzo approaches a Lion Monk who turned out to be a personal advisor to the magistrate. They made short, enlightened small talk, and the monk revealed that the magistrate is spread thin dealing with the problems of a border town. He is constantly solving four issues at once.

We meet with Matsu Koshe, and he points to Yakisoba and asks, "How familiar are you with this region?"

Yakisoba is quite familiar with the region, so he asks if he knows the village of [[Mushi Mura]]. Yakisoba knows that it's a village that throws a yearly festival. The festival is during the first week of Togashi. He had received a missive from [[Akoma Takashi]]. It reads, "Need help. Send it quickly."

We agree to help.
