## Overview
### Exploring the caldera
We encounter a wounded bushi. He's wearing Lion armor. He is [[Ikoma Naomori]]. Yakisoba recognized him and gave him a bite to eat. He asks us our business here and says he is seeking revenge against [[Isawa Tenzin]].

He met and fell in love with a geisha named Gai while he was engaged to Tenzin's sister. Tenzin killed her. Naomori faced him, but was unable to best him.

Yakisoba offers to help him, but turns out the guy was a fucking spirit, and Yakisoba got spirited away.

### The downpour
The rain starts coming down hard. An old man calls out to Junji and Haku. His name is [[Jenchin]]; apparently a peasant. He offers shelter to the two. These fucking guys are tengu, no doubt.

Suddenly, we are surrounded by tengu. One raised his sword in the air and demanded a duel. Junji accepted, of course. He cut one down, and the rest exploded into feathers.

### The shrine
We approach a shrine, where we see two figures exiting. One was wearing an oni mask.
