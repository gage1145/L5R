## Characters
- [[Doji Junji]]
- [[Togashi Hanzo]]
- [[Kuni Yoshiyuki]]
- [[Usagi Haku]]
- [[Akodo Yakisoba]]

## Overview
### The skirmish
Haku swiftly dispatched one of the bandits. Everyone else had issues with these fucking guys. Yoshiyuki shot one of the archers (to death). Haku then killed another one (really holding the team on his shoulders). Haku also killed the last guy! One person escaped, and the group decided to chase him. He is running in the opposite direction the group was travelling. Eventually, we caught up to him and questioned him.

### Questioning the bandit
The bandit informed us that their numbers are maybe a couple dozen. He informed us that a woman matching the description we have passed through the night before, and she had multiple swords on her person. She was heading toward a hill in the distance. Akodo waved the man away once we got the information.

### Tracking the long-haired woman
As we approached the hill, we saw a woman matching our description with a bandage on her left hand. She has the swords in front of her. As she catches Yakisoba's eyes, she quickly raises an axe and shatters the swords. She then puts the axe down, and lowers her head.

Yakisoba asked,

###### "Why?"

She admits that she is in fact [[Seppun Kazumi]]. She stole the blades because they are cursed...She plans on burying them. She says they are cursed with the angry spirit of [[Seppun Daiori]]. 

Yakisoba informs the group that the swords were unrecoverable. We accept this explanation.

### Back in [[Tochigi]]
We return to the estate of [[Daidoji Haruki]] and explain to him that his swords were unrecoverable. Yakisoba prostrated himself for stealing a horse, and Haruki chose exile from his lands as punishment. He gave us our papers through Scorpion lands and nothing else.

### Traveling to Scorpion Lands
We arrived at lake [[Mizu-umi no Fuko]] leading into Scorpion lands on the day of the [[Feast of Lord Bayushi]]. Two days later we made port in lake [[Aka Mizu-umi]] in the [[Keep of the Fortunate Husband]]. They inspected our papers and had absolutely zero questions. We quickly move through the castle and out toward the capital city. We approach a crossroads where a ronin is standing.

### The Ronin
The man is likely wanting to partake in [[Tsuchigiri]]. He wants to challenge one of us with a new technique he has learned. Yoshiyuki approaches the man, anticipating that he wants to duel.

###### "Who are you?"
Asks the ronin. His name is [[Kuma Mitomoro]]. He challenged Yoshiyuki to a duel to the death. Our crab was immediately horribly injured. Seeing this, Usagi threw his tanto at the ronin. Yakisoba swung his katana at the ronin, and cut him in half. Seeing this major breach of etiquette, Hanzo tackled Yakisoba to the ground.
