## Overview
### Speakg with [[Iuchi Gozaiman]]
Gaoxing approaches the lord and broaches the topic of the illness spreading amongst the villagers. He says Gaoxing should speak to the butler, but he doesn't know the names of any of his servants. Gaoxing suspects the monks may be involved in the sickness.

### Quarantining Akodo
Gozaiman demands that Akodo must be quarantined for interacting with the sick servant. Akodo really fucking contests being locked up in quarantine. He makes a big fucking scene about it. Eventually he does actually finally go into the shrine.

### Finding Hanzo
Hanzo wakes up and tells the local samurai that the monks attacked him. They offer to take him to the village healer.

### Yoshiyuki chasing the monks
Yoshiyuki tries chasing after the monks, but is stopped at the gate. The guard says that he had met Crabs before, and that there is an illness in the south that we do not have here. Yoshiyuki points out that the monks should be returned to prevent the spread. [[Ko]] and [[Umi]] have already gone after them on horse. 

Yoshiyuki just goes out of sight and scales the wall. Outside the castle, he interrogates some peasants. They direct him where the monks went.

Eventually Yoshiyuki doesn't find a horse. He does find a heavily injured samurai with a missing arm. This is one of the samurai who chased the monks. The other samurai left with the monks. IT WAS AN INSIDE JOB!!!

Further down the road, he finds another samurai who had obviously killed himself. Against his better judgment, he continues to track the monks. It soon becomes clear that the monks left the road some time ago. He tracks them through the tall grass and eventually finds them.

### The Doctor
Later in the day, the doctor makes his way to the servant quarters. While he's making his way to Hanzo, Gaoxing stops him. He tells him that the people are certainly dying, and he recommends that absolutely no one interact with them.

The doctor goes around clearing the party. Yakisoba gives a stink again. None of us have the stink. 

Gozaiman comes out and tells us to just wait it out until the servants keel over. Togashi eludes to it being a Shadowland disease, but the doctor is unfamiliar with that kind of shit. Haku volunteers to help with the diagnosis.

### Haku visits the ill
Haku determines that it is more likely that the illness is blood magic than not. It is undeniable that these villagers are now extremely dangerous. They should be burned. Worst case, everyone within a 1-mile radius should be burned. Haku also doubts that the disease is not likely to spread.

Haku struggles with making this decision for a while.

### The Monks' Quarters
We search the monks' quarters and find a black pillow book. Akodo opened it, and sees Kanji but can't understand what it is. Gaoxing and Togashi do recognize that whatever this is, it is pure evil. We presented it to Gozaiman and insisted that we go hunt these fuckers.

### Tracking the Monks
The rest of the group eventually comes across the samurai. His name is [[Ko]]. We take his chop as well. It was [[Umi]]. We finally make it to Yoshiyuki.

There is a very elongated discussion on whether we should start a grass fire. Hanzo is convinced this is the best option. Nobody else agrees.

Yoshiyuki ambushes the monks instead.
