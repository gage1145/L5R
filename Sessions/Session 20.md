## Overview

### The approaching army
[[Ichiro Jinsuke]] orders everyone double time back to [[Shiro Ichiro]]. We need to get there before the approaching empire army. It may be that [[Ichiro Masakatsu]] is in this army, and that we killed his double.

We decide to head back to Shiro Ichiro and try to convince [[Ichiro Toshekatsu]] to demand Masakatsu's head as retribution.

Yakisoba, [[Koganshi Onotatsu]], Gaoxing, and Hanzo barge in to the room where Toshekatsu is meeting with [[Moto Kuranosuke]]. We see a man dressed in very elaborate attire, and a man in Badger armor. The elaborate man is screaming at Toshekatsu about pride, treason, chaos... How could the daimyo let things get this bad.

Toshekatsu says he is sorry and that he is the one who took the head of [[Ichiro Shingen]]. He needs a few hours to prepare for his atonement. Hanzo asks about Ichiro Shingen, but he doesn't know the name.

He strongly feels that his uncle intends seppuku, and he has much to prepare for.

### Eavesdropping
Yoshiyuki scales the castle to listen in on the emperor's men. He hears one of the voices complaining about having to wait around and that they should just get moving. He doesn't give af if the daimyo kills himself. The other voice tells him that there is basic decency to consider. All in all not much information was gained.

### Who is Ichiro Shingen?
Gaoxing finds out that Shingen was a retainer employed by Masakatsu. Weird that the emperor would be up in arms about this fucking guy. This information was delivered by a scribe. Last he heard of Shingen was 3 years ago.

### The Seppuku Ceremony of Ichiro Toshekatsu
Tashekatsu informs us that he plans to die tonight. He has taken the blame for us killing.

We think that Masekatsu is probably going to [[The Claws of the Badger]]. Essentially, Masekatsu will be made the gatekeeper of badger lands.

We don't stick around for the ceremony, and decide to leave without saying goodbye to Jinsuke.
