## Overview
### The Battle of the Claws of the Badger
We start at the [[Battle of The Claws of the Badger]]. Our forces led by [[Ichiro Jinsuke]] clashed with [[Ichiro Masakatsu]]'s foces. Yakisoba quickly got into a duel with a Badger ronin and swiftly cut him down.

While the battle was happening, Goaxing left with the retreating imperial army that was stationed at the fort to head off the incoming rifleman battalion. Goaxing realized they are at least an hour away from the battle, so he tried stalling them. He let them know that a battle is taking place in Badger lands, and that

***The daimyo is correcting a situation.***

His goal here is to dissuade them from entering the battle on the side of Masakatsu.

Yaksoba gets into a duel again with [[Ichiro Kenmatsu]], the badger that we held captive back in that village we saved upon entering Badger lands.

As the battle was ending, we found out that Masakatsu fled early on. WHAT A PUSSY! He probably fled north into [[Yobanjin]] territory. We all (excluding Gaoxing) move in that direction. Once Gaoxing makes it back to the battle, he follows his companions.

### The Skirmish of Ichiro Masakatsu
We made haste to where we suspected Masakatsu was fleeing, and soon we saw 4 samurai in the distance. We engaged the men. Yakisoba and Hanzo engaged Masakatsu, Haku drew fire from the archer, and Junji and Yohiyuki engaged one of the sword-bearers.

Yakisoba manages to wound Masakatsu almost mortally, but was immediately pulled off his horse and nearly killed by another samurai. Yoshiyuki engaged that samurai to protect Yakisoba. While they were busy with that samurai, Hanzo engulfed Masakatsu's face in an inferno, killing him decisively. She also pulled off Yohiyuki from his horse and almost killed him.

Haku recognizes the samurai as [[Ichiro Oki]], a samurai who was schooled by the Hare. Haku asks her why she led such a shameful life, and she said she was only serving her lord, Masakatsu. Gaoxing appealed to her humanity, and asked that if she had any information on our other marks, she could save hundreds of people. Unfortunately, she wasn't important enough to know anything useful.

Gaoxing inspects Masakatsu's sword. It is a very nice sword, but other than that, it is nothing particularly special. He recognizes the maker's mark as a high-end swordsmith in [[Otosan Uchi]], named [[Konochiro]]. It was probably purchased for him as a gift. It is heavier than expected.

### Return to [[Shiro Ichiro]]
We return with the head of Masakatsu. Everyone is celebrating. [[Ichiro Jinsuke]] is very appreciative of the work we had done. He asks to talk to Gaoxing alone.

Jinsuke asks what he thinks about him killing himself. His reasoning is that he allowed himself to become a puppet to such a minor, insignificant figure such as Masakatsu and severing the shame of his lineage. 

Gaoxing reassures him that his people see in him...hope. Shame is a difficult thing, but it is an opportunity to grow. He convinces him not to fucking kill himself, and to stay as daimyo of the Badger clan.

Jinsuke handed over a letter to Gaoxing. This letter waws clearly written weeks if not months ago. It commended us on our work in Badger lands. It arranges for our attendance at a tournament in Phoenix lands. It is 7 weeks from now. The message was written by [[Daidoji Kentarō]].

We all look at [[Koganshi Onotatsu]] who seems hesitant to return to Phoenix lands.
