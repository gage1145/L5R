## Summary
The capital of Unicorn lands. It used to be a [[Lion Clan]] fortress, or maybe a [[Fox Clan]] fortress? Built during the dawn of the century by the [[Ki-rin]]. Serves as the party's transit point to [[The City of the Rich Frog]] — boats can be hired here for 3 bu.
