## Summary
A major border city in Lion Clan territory, accessible by boat from [[Shiro Shinjo]] via a river connection. A busy commercial and political hub — the magistrate, [[Hoshe Mitsu]], is said to be constantly managing four problems at once. [[Akodo Kinsume]] commands the city's border forces.

An illegal gun trade is being smuggled through the city, which [[Doji Gaoxing]] flagged to [[Daidoji Kentarō]] as a sign of a possible brewing uprising. The [[Owl Clan]] maintains a dojo here, operated by [[Fukuro Kanamori]]. The party was hosted by [[Matsu Koshe]] upon arrival.
