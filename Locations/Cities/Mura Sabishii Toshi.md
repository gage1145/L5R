## Summary
**Lonely Shore City** (CN13) in the [Sabishii province](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Sabishii_province "Sabishii province") [1](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Mura_Sabishii_Toshi#cite_note-1) is a busy port city, [2](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Mura_Sabishii_Toshi#cite_note-SoulsSteel1-2) one of the [Crane](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Crane_Clan "Crane Clan")'s major shipping hubs, and is one of the centers of their economic web.
