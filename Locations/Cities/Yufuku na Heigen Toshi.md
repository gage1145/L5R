## Summary
**Prosperous Plains City** was a city of the [Crane Clan](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Crane_Clan_\(TCG\) "Crane Clan (TCG)"). A large city in [Daidoji](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Daidoji_family_\(TCG\) "Daidoji family (TCG)") lands and a major economic center for the Crane, it had numerous features to draw travelers. It was home to the [Wind God's Market](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Wind_God%27s_Market "Wind God's Market"), as well as a large shrine to the [Fortune of Roads](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Fortune_of_Roads "Fortune of Roads"), where travelers hoping for a swift or safe journey can pay their respects. [1](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Yufuku_na_Heigen_Toshi_\(TCG\)#cite_note-1) According to some records, the fabled monk which named the [River of the Blind Monk](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/River_of_the_Blind_Monk_\(TCG\) "River of the Blind Monk (TCG)") thwarted a [Scorpion](https://l5r.fandom.com/wiki/Scorpion_Clan_\(TCG\) "Scorpion Clan (TCG)") attempt to grab the city.
