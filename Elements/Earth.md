>## Summary
>Earth was passive strength, resistance, and fortitude. All life blossomed from the earth and one day must return to it, so it was also the element of healing, regeneration and renewal.
> ## Usage
>As it related to spell effects, Earth was used to affect earth and the land in general, minerals, metals, protection and warding, stamina, and willpower. Kuni Shugenja and Tamori Shugenja focused mainly on Earth.
